Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024-11-29

Introduction

WhereIsTime is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information Collection

Important: All data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers.

We collect the following information:

  • Website domains you visit (not the full URLs)
  • Time spent on each website

Required Permissions

Our extension requires the following permissions to function:

  • Storage Permission: Used to save your browsing time statistics locally on your device
  • Tabs Permission: Required to track the currently active website and time spent
  • HTTPS Website Access: Needed to monitor time spent on secure HTTPS websites only
  • Web Navigation (Optional): Used for more accurate time tracking when navigating between pages

Data Usage

The collected data is used solely for:

  • Displaying your website usage statistics
  • Generating time tracking reports
  • Providing insights about your browsing habits

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally using Chrome's secure storage API
  • Data never leaves your device
  • You can delete all stored data at any time through the extension's interface
  • Data is automatically limited to prevent excessive storage usage

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • View your stored data at any time
  • Export your data
  • Delete all your data
  • Disable optional permissions
  • Uninstall the extension at any time

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
